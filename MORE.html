more: friends & influences

here is just a small sampling of people whose work has inspired or influenced me

Who Why
Kait Champagne a personal friend, deeply suportive, has an AA degree in photo. rock climbs religiously, may have a future in tattooing.
Tim Heuser tim runs the photo lab at Unique Photo in Fairfield, NJ, where i get most of my work developed. he primarily shoots black and white, and he is very good at it.
Gerard Exupery gerard asked me to provide edits for his photo book Women Hold Up Half the Sky. his respect and appreciation for my opinion was uplifting, and he gave me a beautiful vintage Nikkormat camera in exchange for my work. he has been doing street photography for almost twice as long as i've been alive.
Fred McDarrah fred was the staff photographer and photo editor at the village voice newspaper for the majority of the time it ran. if there was a historic event in nyc, he probably photographed it. i became intimately familiar with his work when i worked for the archive that owned all of his collected photos, ephemera, books, etc.
Rosalind Fox Solomon another photographer whose collection i worked with. she started at age 40 and is still kickin' today, in her 90s. her work is unflinching, gorgeously developed, and never cropped. she lives in an apartment overlooking WSQ with just a little black cat.
godteeth.com not a photographer or anyone i know personally, but an html site i stumbled upon one day in a fit of web 1.0 nostalgia. i love how he uses his site as a simple platform for thoughts, interests, and self expression. i would love for this, my first html site, to eventually become something more in line with his vision of a website as a commonplace book.
